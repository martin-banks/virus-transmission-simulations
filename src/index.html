Virus transmission simulator

We all know that coming into contact with someone or something carrying a virus has a risk that we can become infected too. But how can we understand that on a larger scale?

In this simulation I want to visualise how a virus can spread through a population and how different factors can alter the rate of growth.

It does not use any data from real virus transmissions and is not inteded as a guide to chart or predict current world events.
